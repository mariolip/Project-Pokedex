Welcome to Project Pokedex!

Project Pokedex is designed to help introduce people young and old to Pokemon in a simple and fun way!

You'll notice a few parts of our website, including the following:

  • A Pokedex to meet all of the Pokemon in the new games, Pokemon Scarlet and Pokemon Violet!
  • A type chart to help you understand types are super effective, not very effective, and immune to each other!
    • This is really important because all Pokemon have a type, and many have two!
  • A "Who's That Pokemon?" game to see which Pokemon you recognize, just like in the show!
  • Quizzes to test your Pokemon knowledge!

You can also log in and track your stats as you learn more and more! Climb the leaderboards and see where you end up!

Note: Project Pokedex is NOT a comphresive website with every bit of Pokemon information. This website is intended to make getting into the series easier

in a fun and interactive way with a Pokedex in similar style that found in Pokemon Scarlet and Pokemon Violet.
